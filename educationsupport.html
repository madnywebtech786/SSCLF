Education Support Programs

Our Education Support Programs aim to make quality education accessible to every child in Southern Sudan, especially in remote and underserved communities. We are committed to reducing illiteracy and ensuring that all children—especially girls—have the tools and support they need to succeed.

  • ✔We help build and refurbish primary schools in villages where no educational infrastructure previously existed.
  • ✔We provide textbooks, notebooks, writing tools, and uniforms to students who would otherwise go without the basics needed to learn.
  • ✔Our programs equip local educators with the training and resources they need to deliver effective, engaging, and inclusive lessons.
  • ✔We offer scholarships and incentives to encourage and support girls’ education, addressing cultural and economic barriers that often hold them back.
  • ✔We promote the importance of early learning and support programs that prepare young children for success in primary school and beyond.
  • ✔We engage families and local leaders in understanding the long-term value of education, helping to shift mindsets and prioritize schooling.
